Last reviewed: 2026-07-22

This document highlights provider-policy risks relevant to TVFinance. It is
not legal advice, does not interpret every applicable term, and is not a
substitute for reading the current
[TradingView Terms of Use, Policies, and Disclaimers](https://www.tradingview.com/policies/).

## Key restrictions

TradingView's terms state, among other things, that:

- TradingView services, features, and APIs may change or be discontinued
  without notice or backward compatibility.
- TradingView and its data providers retain rights in platform content and
  market data.
- Market data is licensed for display-only use, subject to purpose and data
  provider restrictions.
- Non-display uses are restricted. Examples identified by TradingView include
  automated trading, automated order generation, price referencing, order
  verification, algorithmic decision-making, risk management, and other
  machine-driven processing without direct human-readable display.
- Third-party products or services that enable prohibited non-display use are
  also restricted.
- Redistribution, sublicensing, sale, commercial use, and creation of products
  or services based on provider content may require express permission and
  separate data-provider rights.
- TradingView may block access, terminate accounts, investigate suspected
  violations, or pursue other remedies.

The official terms control if this summary is incomplete, outdated, or
inconsistent with them.

## What TVFinance does not provide

TVFinance is independent and unofficial. It is not affiliated with, sponsored
by, endorsed by, or vetted by TradingView. It provides software only and does
not provide:

- permission to access or use TradingView services or data;
- a market-data license or redistribution right;
- an exemption from TradingView or data-provider terms;
- permission for automated, algorithmic, commercial, or non-display use; or
- any assurance that an unofficial interface will remain available.

The presence of a Python API, CLI, cache, WebSocket client, or MCP server is a
technical capability, not evidence that a particular use is permitted. In
particular, do not send TradingView-derived content to an AI system or use it
through MCP unless you have independently established the necessary rights and
permissions.

## User responsibility

Before using TVFinance, review the current official terms and the terms of each
relevant exchange or data provider. Obtain written permission or an appropriate
licensed data feed where required. Do not use TVFinance to bypass accounts,
subscriptions, access controls, rate limits, technical restrictions, or data
licensing requirements.

Do not use TradingView-derived data through TVFinance for automated trading,
order generation, price referencing, algorithmic decisions, risk management,
commercial services, redistribution, or other restricted non-display purposes
without explicit authorization from all relevant rights holders.

If your intended use is not clearly permitted, do not use the TradingView
integration. Use an official or separately licensed market-data API and seek
qualified legal advice for your circumstances.
